Benches

Benches consist of a seat and usually two legs. They can be straight, curved, park style with a seat back, or couch style. The finish can be all polished or all steeled. Benches can be used to identify family burial plots, or used in conjunction with a family monument. Benches are a common choice in areas where people pass or gather. They are an excellent option in areas such as parks and other public places to be used as a memorial or tribute to your loved one.
